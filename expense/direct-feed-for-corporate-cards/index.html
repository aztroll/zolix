Fetch your corporate card feeds directly and securely

When it comes to your financial data, there's no need for middlemen. We offer direct feeds for your corporate cards, so you can fetch your transactions from your card provider and view them instantly in Zoho Expense.

TRY FREE FOR 14 DAYS

What is direct feed integration?

Direct feeds are a way for you to get your corporate card transactions directly from your card provider with no imports or connectors. They'll be streamed securely from your bank to Zoho Expense.

Fetch feeds securely

Your feeds won't be handled by any other parties. They are pulled directly from card providers and projected in Zoho Expense. This means your sensitive financial data is protected at all times.

Increase the efficiency

Reduce the time it takes to fetch corporate card feeds. With direct feed integration, transactions are fetched into Zoho Expense faster.

Fetch feeds from multiple card providers

With direct feeds, you can pull corporate card transactions by integrating with the following card providers—Visa, Mastercard, and Amex.

The best part? Receipts are digitally stored in your Zoho Expense account, so you never have to worry about losing paper copies.
